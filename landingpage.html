    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions about how our lost and found system works? Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive.

    1. How do I report a lost item?

    Click on "Report Lost Item" in the menu and fill out the form with as much detail as possible about your lost item, including when and where you lost it, and any identifying features.

    2. What should I do if I find an item?

    Click on "Report Found Item" and provide details about what you found, where you found it, and when. If the item has identification, you may also want to contact the owner directly if possible.

    3. Is there a fee to use this service?

    Our basic service is completely free. We offer premium plans with additional features, but reporting and searching for lost items is always free.

    4. How are items returned to their owners?

    We facilitate contact between the finder and owner. You can arrange to meet in a public place, use a neutral drop-off location, or in some cases we can assist with verified returns for premium members.

    5. What safety measures are in place?

    We recommend always meeting in public places during daylight hours. Never share personal financial information. Our system includes user verification for added safety.
