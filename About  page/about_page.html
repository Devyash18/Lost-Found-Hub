About Us

Welcome to Lost & Found – your go-to platform for reuniting lost items with their rightful owners. We believe that every lost item has a story and a person missing it.

Our mission is to create a trustworthy community where people can report, search, and recover lost belongings. Whether it's a misplaced phone, a forgotten bag, or a cherished item, our platform connects finders with seekers in the most efficient way possible. We prioritize safety, transparency, and community-driven efforts to help bring peace of mind to those who’ve lost something valuable.
